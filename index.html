Practice Assessments

Graduates First

Graduates First offer a variety of psychometric tests and other job assessment preparation solutions for students including detailed step-by-step guides to over 100 top graduate employer's recruitment stages and hints on how to pass their assessment process.

Shortlist.Me

Shortlist.Me is an interview preparation platform which helps Careers Services transform the way that they facilitate mock interviews. Careers Services are able to support students to reach their full potential through practical application, putting them ahead of the competition in real interviews and increasing employability.

Reel Ability

There’s only so much a traditional CV can communicate. Reel Ability enables you to upload a video alongside this and expand on your bullet points, so you can Reely stand out from the rest. Helping your next employer see the person behind the paper.
